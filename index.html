About me

My name is Aaron Gonzalez and I am a Front End Developer/Designer currently living in Phoenix, Arizona. I really enjoy building elegant and efficient user interfaces that can make a difference in the world.

My professional experience includes wireframing, prototyping and developing those designs into web or mobile applications. I am very comfortable collaborating within a team to create a polished finished product. I love to design marketing material for facebook ads, GIFs for instagram, custom emails and icons/graphics for my web pages. Currently, I am developing an iOS shopping application to create a better shopping experience for our customers.

Other interests include traveling, soccer, yoga, hiking, scuba diving and biohacking. Looking to keep growing both personally and professionally by using these skills to gain more knowledge and experience.
